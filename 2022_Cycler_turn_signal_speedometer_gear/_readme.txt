Two-piece system consisting of bicycle handlebar for indicating turn intention and a vest to increase visibility from behind

The handlebar consisted of two three-way switches for turn intent, two brake switches, two LEDs and a display with speedometer and battery info. The vest was connected to the handlebar via Bluetooth to help increase turn signal visibility from behind.

MCU: ESP32
Script: C++
Components: OLED display, switches, accelerometer
Timeline: 2 months
Team role: code

A rover with a single-joint arm, a claw, and a camera, controlled by an Android app.

The rover implements a rear-wheel drive, front-wheel turn, an arm with a 180-degree rotation and a 180-degre bend, a claw at the end of the arm and a camera. The rover is controlled over an Android app designed in Kodular (https://www.kodular.io/).

MCU: ESP32
Script: C++
Components: ESP32 camera, servo motors, DC motor, motor driver circuit
Timeline: 1 month
Role: circuit design (full), code (full)
